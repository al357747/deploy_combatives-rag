# Clinching Escapes

**Purpose:** Cover escape sequences from the double collar tie.


## Cross-face Escape

1. Post one hand on the opponent's jaw.
2. Head trap the opponent's arm.
3. The other arm monitors the opponent's other arm.

### Counter the Cross-face Escape

1. Post head on cross-face arm.
2. Half moon step
3. Clear the cross-face arm with the far arm from the cross-face arm.

## Double hands to face

Push both hands against your opponent's face

### Counter the Double Hand Escape

Knee wedge to arm triangle

## Shrug Escape

1. One arm pulls on the opponent's head.
2. The other arm breaks the hold of the arm on the same side.
3. Half-moon step to angle out.

## Cross Arm to Elbow

1. One arm to the opponent's cross elbow 
2. The other arm to the opponent's head
3. Half-moon step on the side of the arm that is on the opponent's elbow.
