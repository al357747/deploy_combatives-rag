# Clinching Turning

**Purpose:** Cover turning opponent and clinch escapes


## Turning Opponent 90 degrees


### Double collar tie, two-hand clinch around opponent's head

Turn the opponent 90 degrees either direction

### clinch hold: one hand behind the head and the other at the crook of the elbow of the mirror arm

Turn the opponent 90 degrees in either direction.  Note switch hands if opponents attempts to escape with pummeling.
