# Marching fake teeps

**Purpose:** Develop marching fake teeps into an attack.  March moves the rear or lagging leg forward. These are mid-range knee and elbow combinations.

- Teep, march, knee, elbow
- Teep, march, march, knee, elbow
- Teep, march, march, march, knee, elbow
