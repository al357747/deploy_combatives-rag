# Guard Pull Techniques

**Purpose:**  Demonstrate offensive techniques that pull an opponent's defending arm away from their guard position, creating an opening for punches or elbow strikes.

## Guard Pull off Jab

1. Throw a stiff jab that holds your opponent's rear arm in the guard position for a beat.
2. The jabbing hand then reaches for the opponent's mirror guard arm and secures a grip with the four fingers' gloved pad around the opponent's thumb bone at the palm. 
3. Pull the opponent's guard arm down like an old-fashioned slot machine handle to clear an opening to their head.
4. Throw a left horizontal elbow to their temple or any vulnerable target on their head.

## Guard Pull off Cross


1. Throw a jab, then a stiff cross that holds the opponent's lead arm in guard position for a beat.
2. The cross arm reaches for and grips the opponent's thumb side palm of the lead guard.
3. Pull the opponent's guard arm down like an old-fashioned slot machine handle to clear an opening to their head.
4. Throw a right horizontal elbow to their temple or any vulnerable target on their head.

## Cross Body Guard Pull (Lead Arm)

1. Throw a jab, then a stiff cross that holds the opponent's lead arm in guard position for a beat.
2. Reach your left hand across your body to grip your opponent's lead guard at the thumb side palm.
3. Pull your opponent's guard across his body.
4. Throw a right horizontal elbow to their temple or any vulnerable target on their head.

## Cross Body Guard pull (Rear Arm) 

1. Throw a jab, cross, then a stiff jab that holds your opponent's rear arm in the guard position for a beat.
2. Reach your right hand across your body to grip your opponent's rear guard at the thumb side palm.
3. Pull your opponent's guard across his body.
4. Throw a left horizontal elbow to their temple or any vulnerable target on their head.
