# Superman Punch

**Purpose:** A fast punch that follows immediately behind a leg kick fake.

## Mechanics

1. Lift the rear leg to fake a kick.
2. Pull the leg back.
3. Simultaneously throw a rear cross with body rotation as your rear foot touches the mat.
