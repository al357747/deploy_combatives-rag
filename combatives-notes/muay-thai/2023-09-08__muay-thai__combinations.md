# Basic combinations: 

## three-phase combination

**Purpose:** This combination can break guard, draw reaction, finish with kick, and attack the liver.

Phase one- cross, cross, left kidney shot, Right leg kick

Phase two- Right leg kick

Phase Three- Fake right kick, left hook, Right leg kick


## Top and bottom bag combination

jab, Cross, right slip, left slip, left hook


## Combination three

cross, pull, Cross, left hook
