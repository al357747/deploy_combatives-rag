# Elbow Combinations

**Purpose:** These combinations set up elbow strikes.


## Teep setup for Spin Elbow

1. Setup combination:  Teep

2. Combination: Show front teep, plant foot for a lead spear elbow, lead step to the right for a spin rear elbow (or spin rising low elbow).


## Jab hold setup for a spin elbow

1. Jab hold to opponent's guard, jab arm to spear elbow, follow with horizontal rear elbow, rear step across to the left side for a spin left elbow (or spin rising low elbow).
