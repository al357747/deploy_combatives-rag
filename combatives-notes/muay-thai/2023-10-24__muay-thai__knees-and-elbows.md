# Pairing Knees and Elbows

**Purpose:** Pairing knees and elbows in combinations.

- Rear long knee to horizontal right elbow to double guard

- Switch knee to left spear elbow

- Soccer ball throw hand movement to hold opponent's guard, then knee, followed by slot machine arm pull to clear path for the same arm horizontal elbow to the head.
