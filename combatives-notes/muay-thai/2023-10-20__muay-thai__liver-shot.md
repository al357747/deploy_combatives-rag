# Liver Shot

**Purpose:** Discuss the liver shot mechanics and combos using the liver shot

## Mechanics

1. Lead leg steps in line.

2. Lean torso to load.

3. Liver punch is an angle between a body hook and an uppercut.  Or angle from the liver up to their left shoulder.

4. Stick the punch with the intent of reaching to grab their liver.

5. From the liver punch, follow up with one of the following:

- Right leg kick

- Switch knee to the liver

- Switch kick to the liver

- Right spear elbow to the face between your opponent's guard.

## Combinations involving a liver punch

- Jab, cross, liver shot, follow-up

- Jab, cross, hook chained with a jab, cross, liver shot, follow-up

- Jab, cross, hook, uppercut, liver shot, follow-up

## Defense from the Liver shot 

Curl torso to the right just enough for you elbow of the right guard to cover the liver.  DO NOT bring the guard down to the hip.
